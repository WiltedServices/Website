Our Philosophy

Our Mission.

We believe that information should be universal, unrestricted, and inherently accessible to anyone with a connection.

Unrestricted by Design.

The internet was built as a decentralized, resilient network meant to survive and thrive without bottlenecks. Today, institutional firewalls and corporate filters have created a fragmented web. Wilted Services exists to bridge those gaps.

We are a collective of independent developers, engineers, and digital rights advocates who prioritize technical excellence over bureaucratic compliance.

Privacy

Zero logging. Absolute discretion. Our tools are designed to leave no trace, ensuring that your digital footprint remains yours alone.

Integrity

Open-source where it matters. We value transparency and invite the community to peer-review our security implementations.
