Android Controls

A place where you don’t have to live by the restrictions of a screen.
▶Private Browser on Android (Settings WebView exploit)
  • On your mobile Android device, open up the settings app.
  • Scroll down to the “About Phone” section
  • Open “legal information” then “Samsung legal”
  • Depending on your device, in the options that show up, there should be articles built into your settings app
  • In the articles, try to find any link to samsung.com
  • On the Samsung site, scroll down to the bottom and click the YouTube social link
  • On YouTube, search for google.com, and click on the link in the description of the first suggested video
  • Now you’re on Google in your settings app, Enjoy!
▶Family Safety on Android
  • Open up the settings app Search or find “Accessibilities”
  • Then at the bottom, click “Installed Apps” and find the Family Safety App
  • In this, you can turn off the app’s permissions, which removes everything except for the built in Web Monitoring on Edge
  • You can also set a shortcut so that whenever you press a certain button they toggle on/off
▶Qustodio for Android
  • Go into setting onto your Android, and find or search for “pin app”
  • Turn it on, then go into your recent apps, hold down on settings, and pin the app
  • Go into specific permissions that Qustodio needs to function, and you can turn them on/off whenever.
