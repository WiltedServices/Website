▶Unblocked Browser

  • Android Device

  • Connected to internet with editable DNS settings.

Disadvantages:

  • Can't open installed apps.
  • Might be a little buggy.

Advantages:

  • Can use websites like TikTok or Discord.

Instructions:

  1. Open the "Settings" on your Android device if its not blocked when on lockdown.
  2. Navigate to "Wi-Fi" settings.
  3. Tap the cog wheel icon next to your network.
  4. Select "View More" or "Advanced".
  5. Tap "IP Settings" and choose "Static".
  6. In the first DNS box, enter 150.136.163.0. (dns from incognito kajig)
  7. In the second DNS box, enter 0.0.0.0.
  8. Back out and tap "Sign into Network".
  9. Select "Browse Incognito" and leave the browser open. Then, return to Wi-Fi settings.
  10. Change IP Settings back to "DHCP" and tap "Save".
  11. Go back to the browser, and the site will be unblocked.
